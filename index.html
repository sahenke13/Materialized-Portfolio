About Me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer with a natural curiosity and desire to create within both the digital and physical realms. A strong team player, I pride myself on my ability to effectively communicate technical concepts across a variety of skill levels, which is something I developed from my years within a technical sales and support role.

I have a civil engineering background including a Bachelor of Science from the University of Minnesota, and I have recently graduated from the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp through the University of Minnesota. Additionally, I am proficient in HTML, CSS, SASS, JavaScript, React JS, BootStrap, Materialize, Jquery, terminal, Node JS, MongoDB, MySQL, Java, Git and GitHub, and I am always eager to learn more.

When I am not working on further enhancing my coding craft, you can find me enjoying time with my family, the wood shop, designing and building functional home design products or in nature, enjoying the great outdoors.
